about
emily villarreal is a brooklyn-based writer, intimacy coordinator, and actor who recently graduated from the nyu gallatin school of individualized studies with a concentration in the sociology of staged performance. when she's not doing stand-up or on a set, she can be found making puppets or lost in a good book. inspired by the textures and sounds of childhood, emily describes her approach to art as "discerningly light hearted' and centers her creative practice around embracing the world's softer edges.
